We're not in the buzzkill business.

Throughout a typical cycle, there are days where we feel energized, creative, and exuberant. It's on these days that we feel invincible, outgoing, and open to new experiences. For these parts of your cycle, we have selected special strains that gently enhance this bright sunny mood to keep those creative juices flowing. Tokn works with your cycle to keep you on cloud 9, instead of mellowing out your most energized time of the month.

Bring your best self, even on your worst days

We know the drill - cramps, mood swings, anxiety. The lows of each month can leave us exhausted at best and downright ruined at worst. We can't wave a magic wand and make it all disappear, but...Tokn can help take the edge off. On hormonal, emotional days, Tokn helps level out the emotional rollercoaster. Physical symptoms are dulled, and we sprinkle in a little enery boost for good measure.

Customized to you

Each toke features a strain complements the highs and lows of your cycle.

Everybody is different, so why aren't there products tailored to us individually that help us chill out, feel awesome, clear the fog, or reduce cramps? We think that's messed up, so we created a custom solution that works with your cycle to bring out your best.

Small but mighty

You only need a little bit each day. Tokn doesn't outshine you, and there are no roaches or half-eaten goodies - we like to think it's just enough.

If there's one thing we know the world needs, it's tiny jay's and edibles. But don't be fooled by their size, each toke is truly a one hit wonder. The perfect addition to your ritual - and hey, it comes with some health and balance perks, too!
