Token is a personalized monthly microdose kit with a different strain for each day of your cycle.

Find Out More

We're not in the buzzkill business.

Throughout a typical cycle, there are days where we feel energized, creative, and exuberant. It's on these days that we feel invincible, outgoing, and open to new experiences. On these days, we have selected special strains that gently enhance this bright sunny mood to keep those creative juices flowing. Token works with your cycle to keep you on cloud 9, instead of mellowing out your most energized time of the month.

Bring your best self, even on your worst days

We know the drill - cramps, mood swings, anxiety. The lows of each month can leave us exhausted at best and downright ruined at worst. We can't wave a magic wand and make it all disappear, but...
Token can help take the edge off. On hormonal, emotional days, Token helps level out the emotional rollercoaster. Physical symptoms are dulled, and we sprinkle in a little enery boost for good measure.

Customized to you

Each toke features a strain complements the highs and lows of your cycle.

Everybody is different, so why aren't there products tailored to us individually that help us chill out, feel awesome, clear the fog, or reduce cramps? We think that's messed up, so we created a custom solution that works with your cycle to bring out your best.

Made with Love

By women, for women! We hope each box brings you the joy and balance we felt when making this product

Your hormones change every day, so Tokn uses your cycle information to create the ideal combination of daily tokes. We also know that there are morning and midnight tokers, so we take that into consideration when choosing the strains for you.

Small but mighty

You only need a little bit each day. Tokn doesn't outshine you, and there are no roaches or half-eaten goodies - we like to think it's just enough.

If there's one thing we know the world needs, it's tiny jay's and edibles. But don't be fooled by their size, each toke is truly a one hit wonder. The perfect addition to your ritual - and hey, it comes with some health and balance perks, too!
